Mom's Tomato Salad

"Here is a fast, pretty, salad that we love," says Diana. "The boys will only eat it with chips as they think of it as salsa. They freak at raw tomatoes. This salad tastes good just after making it as well as the next day. Good with pasta, chicken and meat dishes."

In a serving bowl mix:
2-5 tomatoes, chopped into large chunks
Green bell pepper, chopped
Whole onion, diced
Flat parsley, chopped
Celery, chopped (optional)

Dress salad with:
Olive oil
Apple cider vinegar
Juice from half a lemon
Ground pepper
Salt

Toss all and serve.
