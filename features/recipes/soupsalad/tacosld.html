Gloria Pitzer's Taco Salad

This quick and easy recipe comes to us from Gloria Pitzer, the queen of copycats and rip-offs. She may not be a genuine Cluff, but she's a Cluff at heart. I mean, a packet of taco seasoning--how brilliant is that?! And you can't argue with dinner for 10 in less than 7.5 minutes. For more rip-off recipes go to the 'Top Secret Recipes' link at the bottom of the Cluff Family Favorites home page.

1 head lettuce, chopped into bite sizes
16 oz bag of shredded cheddar cheese
2 large chopped tomatoes
15 oz can red kidney beans
15 oz bag Doritos nacho cheese tortilla chips
1 lb ground beef or turkey, browned and drained
1 envelope taco seasoning
8 oz bottle of Catalina dressing
Optional: olives, sour cream, guacamole, salsa

Combine lettuce, cheese, tomatoes and beans tossing lightly together in a deep salad bowl. Combine browned meat with taco seasoning mix. Arrange chips, meat and salad in layers and then drizzle with yummy Catalina dressing. Serves 8-10 nicely.
