Melanie's Perfect Pasta Salad

This ohhh-so-scrumptious salad is handy when the vegetable crisper needs cleaning out because the only vegetable it doesn't contain is a rutabaga (but what good recipe does)? There are no set proportions here. Just follow your instincts.

Pasta, cooked and drained (shells, rotini, macaroni etc)
Cheese, cut into small cubes (pepper jack is best)
Red & yellow bell peppers, sliced thin
Artichoke hearts, quartered
Kidney beans, drained
Carrots, grated
Broccoli, cut into small bits
Peas (frozen or fresh)
Olives, sliced
Red Cabbage, shredded
Pimentos
Pepperincinis with juice
Italian dressing Salami strips (optional)

Combine all ingredients and drizzle with Italian dressing. Chill. Makes a big ol' bowl.
