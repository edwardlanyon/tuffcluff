Peanut Butter Balls

This sticky treat was a Cluff childhood favorite. Knowing this, Diana used the balls as stealth vehicles to hide/sneak in herbs, medicines and other vile things the family didn't like.

Peanut butter
Honey
Granola/nuts/raisins
Powdered milk (if too sticky)

Put a glob of peanut butter into a Tupperware container then add honey, granola, nuts and raisins. Mix well. Eat by the spoonful or make into little bite-sized balls.
