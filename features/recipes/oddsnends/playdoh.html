Play Dough

There are two recipes here. Try them both and see which one you like best.


Play dough #1
2 cups flour
1 cup salt
2 cups water (with food coloring)
4 teaspoons cream of tartar
2 tablespoons cooking oil

Mix dry ingredients in saucepan and add liquid. Cook over medium heat until mixture pulls from sides and forms a dough. Knead and keep in airtight container in fridge. Makes a lot (cut in ½ or ¼ for smaller batch).


Play dough #2
1 ½ cups water
½ cup salt
2 tablespoons alum
1 tablespoon oil
2 ½ cups flour
Food coloring

Heat water and salt to 180º in saucepan. Add alum, oil, flour and food coloring. Mix well and allow to cool then knead. Store in an airtight container.
