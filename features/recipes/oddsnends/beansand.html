Bean Sandwich

Yes, this really is as disgusting as it sounds! Just try explaining to your friends at the school lunch table what that brown, gooey substance oozing out of your round bread is. "All of my children shuddered when I tried to serve them," says a bewildered Diana. "But I grew up with bean sandwiches and so did Dad. It's best to serve them only when the children are starving or have been fasting for a long time, otherwise they won't eat them. And who knows, they may try them and find they love them as I still do."

Beans, see Moms Bluewater Beans
Salad dressing/mayonnaise or butter
Bread

Add mashed beans and salad dressing/mayonnaise together for the filler between two slices of bread and hey presto!
