Ants on a Log

This was another Cluff childhood favorite. It a good, healthy high-energy snack for imaginative kids.

Celery stalks
Peanut butter
Raisins

Spread a generous amount of peanut butter into the concave side of a stalk of celery. Place a row of raisins on top (single file) down the stalk. And there you have 'ants on a log'.
