Blackberry Topping

This recipe is the easiest and tastiest thing in the world.

Blackberries
Sugar

Pick and wash blackberries then put them into a bowl. Sprinkle sugar over berries then crush them together. Taste test for sweetness, adding more sugar till it's just right. Serve over homemade ice cream or store-bought French vanilla if in a pinch. If you are a senior citizen, eat the ice cream with saltine crackers.
