Melanie's Yummy Bierrocks

Bierrocks are basically hot German rolls filled with meat, cheese, cabbage and onions. Delicious!

Use frozen dough or...
Basic roll dough:
1 package yeast
¼ cup warm water
1 egg
1 cup milk
¼ cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
¼ cup butter/margarine
3 ½ cups flour

Soften yeast in water. Combine eggs, milk, sugar, salt and butter. Cool to lukewarm. Add 1 ½ cups flour. Add yeast mix. Add remaining flour. Let rise 1 ½--2 hours.

Filling:
½ lb ground beef/turkey
1 cup onions
3 cups chopped cabbage (or sauerkraut)
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon pepper
Dash salt
(Add more vegies, if you like)

Brown meat, add onions and cabbage, cover and cook for 15 minutes. Add salt and pepper then fold in cheese. Allow to cool. When dough is done rising, roll ¼ inch thick and cut into squares. Put filling in each square and pinch shut so they are round like a bun. Set on a greased pan (pinched side down) and let rise for 20 minutes. Bake 20 minutes at 375°. Serves 4-6.
