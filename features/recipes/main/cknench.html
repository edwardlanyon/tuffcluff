Chicken Enchiladas

3 or 4 skinless chicken breasts, cooked and chopped (set aside)

Sauté in butter or margarine:
1 large chopped onion
2 Tbsp. diced green chili peppers

In a medium mixing bowl combine:
1 3 oz. package of cream cheese, softened, (I like to add some shredded cheese too.)
1 Tbsp. milk
¼ tsp. cumin

Combine onion mixture and chicken. Spoon about 3 Tbsp. portions of the mixture into:
12 medium-large tortillas

Roll up filled tortillas and place them, seam side down, in a greased 3 quart baking dish.
In a bowl combine:
1 10 ¾ oz. can of condensed mushroom soup
1 8 oz. sour cream
1 cup of milk
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. oregano
And remaining diced chilies

Pour this soup mixture evenly over the enchiladas in the baking dish. Cover with foil and bake in a 350º oven for about 35 minutes or until heated through. Remove the foil and sprinkle enchiladas with shredded Jack or Cheddar cheese. Return to the oven and bake about 5 more minutes or until the cheese melts.
