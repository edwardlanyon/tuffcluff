Mom's Chicken & Broccoli Casserole

Chicken breasts
Butter
8-10 oz broccoli
1 can cream of mushroom soup
4 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon lemon juice
Sliced almonds, dry Chinese noodles or corn chips
Shredded cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 350º. Boil or bake chicken in butter. Cut or tear chicken into bite-sized pieces and add remaining ingredients to casserole pan. Top with almonds, noodles or chips. Finish with cheese and cook for 30 minutes, cover with foil and then another 10 minutes.
