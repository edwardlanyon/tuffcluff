Emily Brumble's Green Chili Tacos

The secret to these tacos are the green chilis--so don't leave them out.

2-3 lbs. ground meat (beef/turkey)
1 onion
2 bottles mild green chili salsa
2 cans tomatoes
Lettuce, chopped
Cheese, shredded
Sour cream (optional)
Taco shells

Sauté meat with onion. Pour in green chilis and tomatoes. Simmer till nearly dry, stirring often. Fill shells and top with lettuce, cheese and sour cream. Yummy.
