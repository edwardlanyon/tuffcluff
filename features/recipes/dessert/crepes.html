Melanie's Crepes


1 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon lemon extract

Toppings:
Lemon & powdered sugar
Ice cream & chocolate
Ice cream & berries
Fruit
Syrup
Whipped cream

Mix together using a blender for best results. Pour batter into a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and cook till golden brown. Add toppings. For savory crepes leave out the sugar and lemon, but add a pinch of salt.
