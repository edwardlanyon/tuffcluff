Mom's Brilliant Blackberry Crisp

Kids always seem to lose track of time in the summer--but not at the Cluff house! You could always tell it was August because the Cluff kids couldn't come out to play--instead they wandered around with little perma-purple bleeding fingers and, despite the 110º heat, long-sleeved shirts, jeans and moon boots. It was blackberry harvest time! Every hot August hour of daylight was spent slaving away, picking bucket upon bucket--stripping every last blackberry bush within a twelve-mile radius of the house. And at the end of the day the ONLY thing that made it all worthwhile was Mom's brilliant blackberry crisp (and maybe homemade ice cream with crushed blackberries, too).

Preheat oven to 350º
Grease a long pan then fill it with:
6 cups blackberries
1 cup sugar (on top of the fruit)

In a bowl mix:
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup flour
1 cup oatmeal
1 cube margarine or butter
1 tablespoon cinnamon

Pour mixture over the fruit. Bake till bubbly (about 20-25 minutes). Best served warm with homemade vanilla ice cream. Yummy!
