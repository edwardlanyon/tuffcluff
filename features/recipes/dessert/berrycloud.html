Tiana's Berries in a Cloud

This recipe comes from Tiana and, though we didn't actually grow up on it, is much loved. See what you think.

1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese, softened
¼ cup sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
½ cup whipping cream, whipped
¼ cup flaked coconut
Blueberries

Mix cheese, sugar, juice and peel till well blended. Fold in whipped cream and coconut. Spoon into muffin tin cups. With back of spoon, shape into a nest or shell. Freeze. Before serving, let stand in fridge 10 min. Fill shells with berries. Double for a more substantial batch.
