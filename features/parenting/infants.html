Fall Theme: Communicating
Communicating, already? It's hard to believe that as soon as that precious baby takes his first breath, he begins to communicate with you. He has actually been doing it for quite some time inside you. Every movement, tone, every kiss your baby is getting to know you. You are teaching him with every touch. He learns that when he crys, you are there. He learns when he feels uncomfortable, you are there to help him fix the problem and make things better. You are teaching him he can rely on you, you are always there for him and always will be. As he grows and begins to make sounds, gurgles and reach, it is most important for you to react with the same sounds or other happy sounds to let him know you hear and will answer. This teaches him he can talk to you always, that he is interesting to you and that he can share with you. Communication is one of the main facets of life. We all must learn it and it starts now.
