Fall Theme: Communicating
Now that your little one has begun to walk around and make words out of those first sounds he was making a few short months ago, one of the favorite things to do is mimic everything you say and do. Have you ever watched him pick up a remote and put it to his shoulder and talk to it like a cell phone? To learn to communicate with your toddler it's crucial to get down on their level and play with them.
